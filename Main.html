Your Mental Health Does Matter...

About

Mental Health has became important issue in recent years, as it is not the new thing to us but the awareness or importance given by people to this issue is so low. The concern which we give to physical health mainly lacks when it comes to mental illness.Around 13% of worlds population suffer from this mental disorders.

Looking at this it has became necessity to spread awareness about the mental health.

So we 'Mindfulness' team is doing the same work to spread the awareness.On this page we are sharing information on some of the most common mental disorders and we are not just giving information about the disorder but we have also provided some cures to keep your mental health healthy.

Context

Following are some of the most common mental disorders:

  • Depression
  • Panic Disorder
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorder
  • Social Anxiety Disorder
  • Bipolar Disorder
